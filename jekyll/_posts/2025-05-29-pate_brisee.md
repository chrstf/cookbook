for 28 cm form

# Ingredients

Amount | Item
-------|-----
250 g      | flour
175 g | butter
25 g | milk
1 | egg yellow
10 g | sugar
5 g | salt

# Recipe Preparation

1. Mix everyting together until combined
2. set aside at 4--8 °C (fridge) for at least 2 hours
